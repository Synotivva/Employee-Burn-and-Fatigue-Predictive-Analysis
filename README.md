# Employee-Burn-and-Fatigue-Predictive-Analysis
This work provides an exploratory analysis of employee workload, fatigue, and burn indicators across demographics, company type, work-from-home availability, and seniority. It highlights distributions, relationships, and potential risk areas, while handling missing values and outliers to ensure robust insights.
##Business Questions focus on identifying risks and optimizing employee well-being with respect to Workplace Efficiency, Policy Impact, Operational Risks and Retention
##Predictive Analysis Strategy utilizes both Correlation ( to find the strength of the relationship between Resource Allocation and Mental Fatigue. A high positive correlation (r > 0.7) would suggest that adding more resources directly increases fatigue) and Regression ( a predictive model where Mental Fatigue is the dependent variable. Independent variables would include Designation, Resource Allocation, and WFH Setup to predict future burnout risk for new hires) .
